Family therapy

Most things work in your family — and still it sometimes gets stuck. With me you get a safe place to talk calmly and understand one another.

I see the family as part of the solution and the interaction as the key factor for change.

Examples: reach a teenager who withdraws, find a new way for an adult child and parent to meet, or ease tensions between siblings.

About me

Hi, I'm Aysegül

Frequently asked questions

How do sessions with you work?

We always start with a free 30-minute consultation call online to understand your situation and needs. Then we book a 90-minute first session — at my office, online, or as a home visit. The first session is free. If you want to continue, you choose one of three packages.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Often the same week.

Can you come to us?

Yes. Home or office visits can be booked.

Who can come to therapy?

I work with individuals, couples, and families. You are welcome exactly as you are — by yourself, with your partner, or together with a family member, a sibling, or your children.

I'm in another time zone — can we still have sessions?

Yes, absolutely. I offer sessions online no matter where in the world you are.

Where can I read more about your qualifications?

You can find my professional background on LinkedIn.

I want to get in touch — how do I do that?

Email me at info@mindful-homes.se or call and I will get back to you.
